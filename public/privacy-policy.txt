CoreGuard Mobility – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

CoreGuard Mobility (“CoreGuard,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your personal data in line with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 (DPA) and the regulations issued by the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal data.



1. What Personal Data We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

 Name
 Email address
 Contact form messages
 Purchase or preorder details

B. Information Collected Automatically

When you use our site, we may collect:

 IP address
 Device and browser information
 Site activity data (pages visited, time spent)
 Cookies and similar technologies

This is primarily for analytics, performance, and improving user experience.



2. Lawful Basis for Processing (DPA Section 30)

We process personal data under the following lawful bases:

 Consent: Where you voluntarily provide information (e.g., contact forms).
 Performance of a Contract: When processing is necessary for preorders or service fulfilment.
 Legitimate Interests: Improving site functionality and security.
 Compliance with Legal Obligations: When required by Kenyan law.



3. How We Use Your Personal Data

We may use your data to:

 Provide and improve our Services
 Respond to inquiries
 Manage preorders or service requests
 Send product updates or operational notifications
 Maintain website security and functionality
 Comply with statutory obligations

We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.



4. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share data with:

 Trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics)
 Regulatory authorities if legally required
 Business partners supporting fulfilment of our products

Any thirdparty service providers must comply with the Kenya DPA and maintain appropriate data protection measures.



5. CrossBorder Data Transfers (DPA Section 48)

If personal data is transferred outside Kenya, we ensure:

 The receiving country has adequate data protection safeguards, or
 Appropriate contractual safeguards (e.g., data protection agreements) are in place.



6. Data Retention (DPA Section 39)

We retain personal data only for as long as it is necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Once no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymized.



7. Your Rights Under the Kenya DPA

You have the right to:

 Request access to the personal data we hold about you
 Request correction or deletion where appropriate
 Object to processing based on legitimate interest
 Withdraw consent at any time
 Request data portability where feasible
 Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC)

Requests can be submitted to the email below.



8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies for functionality and analytics. You may adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, though some website features may not work as intended.



9. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from loss, unauthorized access, or misuse. However, no digital system is completely secure.



10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes. Updates will be posted with a revised “Last Updated” date.
