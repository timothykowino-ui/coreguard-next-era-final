CoreGuard Mobility — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. What Personal Data We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Contact form messages
  • Purchase or preorder details

B. Information Collected Automatically

When you use our site, we may collect:

  • IP address
  • Device and browser information
  • Site activity data (pages visited, time spent)
  • Cookies and similar technologies

2. Lawful Basis for Processing (DPA Section 30)

  • Consent — where you provide information voluntarily.
  • Performance of a Contract — for preorders or service delivery.
  • Legitimate Interests — improving functionality and security.
  • Legal Obligations — compliance with Kenyan law.

3. How We Use Your Personal Data

  • Provide and improve services
  • Respond to inquiries
  • Manage preorders and service requests
  • Send product updates or notifications
  • Maintain site security

We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share data with trusted service providers (hosting, analytics), regulators where required, and business partners who support fulfilment. Third parties are expected to comply with Kenya DPA safeguards.

5. Cross-Border Data Transfers (DPA Section 48)

When transferring data outside Kenya, we ensure adequate safeguards or contractual protections are in place.

6. Data Retention (DPA Section 39)

We retain data only as necessary for the purpose collected or as required by law, then securely delete or anonymize it.

7. Your Rights Under the Kenya DPA

  • Access the personal data we hold
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request portability
  • Lodge a complaint with the ODPC

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies for functionality and analytics. You may adjust browser settings to refuse cookies, but some features may not work as intended.

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard personal data; however, no system is completely secure.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally — changes will be reflected with a revised “Last Updated” date.
